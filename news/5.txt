Shiromani Akali Dal MLA Manjinder Sirsa has alleged that actors including Deepika Padukone, Ranbir Kapoor and Shahid Kapoor were in a "drugged state" at a party hosted by Karan Johar. "I raise my voice against #DrugAbuse," he wrote. Criticising him, Congress leader Milind Deora replied, "My wife was...present that evening...Nobody was in a 'drugged state' so stop spreading lies."
